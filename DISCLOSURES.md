# Disclosures

OpenRisk maintains neutral positioning. This document declares all relationships, conflicts, and data provenance that may be relevant.

## Commercial Relationships

OpenRisk has no commercial relationships with any of the 20 listed protocols. No protocol pays or has paid for inclusion, ranking, or any other treatment in the registry.

## Feed Provider Relationships

OpenRisk has no paid relationships with any of the 16 listed feed providers. Feed inclusion is based on methodology diversity and public availability of data, not commercial arrangement.

## Data Provenance

- **Coverage data**: The initial seed coverage matrix was imported from the [Neutral Risk](https://github.com/rails-finance/neutral-risk) reference registry at commit `56bd9c4`. This was done as an open-data reference import, not as a partnership. Both projects are open-source AGPL-3.0 public goods targeting the same Ethereum Foundation RFP.
- **Live metrics**: TVL and volume data are fetched from the public [DefiLlama API](https://defiLlama.com/docs/api).
- **Governance facts**: Sourced from public governance forums, protocol documentation, and provider pages. Provenance tags on each fact record the specific source URL and capture date.
- **Audits and incidents**: Sourced from public audit reports and incident post-mortems published by protocols and security firms. Sources are linked in each record.

## Independence

OpenRisk is proposed for stewardship by PWN DAO Foundation. PWN is a DeFi lending ecosystem but is not one of the 20 seed protocols. No listed protocol or feed provider has editorial control over the registry. The no-composite-scoring constraint is binding and documented in the [project charter](./PROJECT_CHARTER.md).

## Reference to Neutral Risk

The OpenRisk registry was built with awareness of, and initial seed data from, the Neutral Risk open-source reference registry. Both projects aim to provide neutral DeFi risk aggregation as public goods. OpenRisk's data model, feed registry, and coverage methodology were developed independently, informed by the Ethereum Foundation RFP specification.

## Conflicts of Interest

No commercial relationship with a listed seed protocol or feed provider is declared in the proposal inputs. PWN DAO Foundation must complete and record a formal conflict check before grant submission or engagement.
